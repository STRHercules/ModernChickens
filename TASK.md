Mounts and Mobility – Add some fun by letting players ride or use chickens for travel. A Giant Chicken Mount could be a tameable breed (perhaps breeding a chicken with a horse?). Riding a tall chicken around would be hilarious and useful – maybe it has a fluttering glide ability (like how chickens slow-fall; the mount could prevent fall damage). This idea integrates well with exploration: players might search for the ingredients to create a Chocobo-like chicken mount. Additionally, you could introduce an “Eggsplosive” Weapon – a handheld Chicken Cannon that shoots eggs or even chickens as projectiles (borrowing the concept from some old mods’ easter eggs). It would be a fun combat utility, turning excess eggs into ammunition that could damage mobs or apply effects. These features aren’t about progression so much as “fun extras” that make the mod feel lively and unpredictable when exploring or fighting.
