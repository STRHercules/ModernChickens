Summoning & Familiars – Add new mobs or summonable creatures via chicken items. A fun example: Rotten Eggs that spawn a Cockatrice. In the Ice and Fire mod, a rare Rotten Egg from chickens can hatch a tame Cockatrice; you could incorporate a similar mechanic. Perhaps if a chicken is kept in gloom or fed a rotten feed, it lays a Cursed Egg that has a chance to spawn a hostile Cockatrice or some spectral chicken monster when thrown. This would add an exploration/combat twist to chicken farming – players might build a dark coop for a chance at summoning a powerful pet or mob.
