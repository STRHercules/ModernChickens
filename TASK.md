Mana Eggs & Avian Mana Pool – Just as Flux Eggs introduced RF power, you could add Mana Eggs for magic power. If Botania is installed, a Mana Chicken could convert its life force into Mana stored in special eggs. An Avian Mana Pool (a mystical version of the flux converter) would accept Mana Eggs and release mana to nearby mana pools or devices. (The concept is similar to the Botania Mana Fluid addon, which turns Botania’s mana into a transferable fluid.) This integration would let players generate or transfer mana using their chicken farm – a huge boon for magic-focused modpacks.
