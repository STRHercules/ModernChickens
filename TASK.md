Industrial Incubators & Gene Splicing – Add high-tech machines that let players bypass or enhance breeding. For example, a Gene Splicing Incubator could consume RF (or Forge Energy) to instantly create or upgrade chickens using gene samples or DNA, similar to how Gendustry let you genetically manipulate Forestry’s bees. This end-game machine might require rare materials and power, but would reward the player with custom-stat chickens or even entirely new breeds without manual breeding. It provides a late-game automation path for chicken breeding, satisfying tech players who love complex machinery.
