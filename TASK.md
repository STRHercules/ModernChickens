Cross-Mod Material Integration – Modern Chickens already dynamically adds any detected metal as a chicken. You can take compatibility further by covering other mod materials and energies. For instance: if Mekanism is present, add chickens for Refined Obsidian, Lithium, or Pellets of RTG (for radioactivity fun); if Thermal Series is present, add chickens for all its alloys (Signalum, Enderium, etc.) or for RF Coils (maybe a Fluxed Electrum Chicken). For Applied Energistics 2, perhaps a Certus Quartz Chicken or even an ME Chicken that produces Matter Balls or singularities. The more mods detected, the more unique chickens or interactions unlock – ensuring Modern Chickens feels seamlessly integrated in any kitchen-sink modpack. This “mod synergy” could even include Resourceful Bees (if installed, maybe allow a hybrid hive where bees can pollinate chickens to boost their production, or a Honey Chicken that produces honeycombs). The key is to make chickens relevant for all major resource types in other mods, so no matter what mods are in play, players can choose a chicken-powered route.
