Blood Magic & Alchemy – Integrate chickens into dark magic mods. For example, a Blood Chicken that passively produces Life Essence (the fluid used in Blood Magic’s altars) by laying “Blood Eggs.” These eggs could be placed in a special altar or Blood Egg Converter to release life essence into the Blood Magic network. It’d provide an alternate (but perhaps less efficient) source of LP for Blood Magic users. Additionally, you could allow chickens to be used in rituals – e.g. sacrificing a chicken could give extra ritual strength, reflecting classic tropes of magic (similar to how some mods use animal sacrifices).
